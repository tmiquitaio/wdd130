Maputo

Maputo is the capital and biggest city of Mozambique, as well as the country’s leading financial, business, and commercial hub. Situated in the southern part of the nation along the western coast of Maputo Bay on the Indian Ocean, the city acts as Mozambique’s primary gateway for trade and travel. Often called the “City of Acacias,” Maputo is admired for its mix of historic landmarks, diverse architectural styles, and a lively culture that blends African traditions with a cosmopolitan atmosphere.
