Salmon River Float

Class I - II | Easy

Big Mallard Adventure

Class III - IV | Intermediate

Our most popular option! Strike the perfect balance between scenic beauty and rolling, splashing fun. This full-day run offers heart-pumping drops mixed with quiet pools where you can swim and recover.
